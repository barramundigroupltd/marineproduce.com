Nutritional Information

Home / Our Fish / Nutritional Information

Barramundi contains the following goodies to keep you and your family healthy and strong! 

Omega 3: Which helps to reduce heart disease, reduce depression symptoms and curb joint pain. 

Selenium: An antioxidant which is proven to improve immune system, reduce the occurrence of prostate cancer, cardiovascular disease and help to reduce cognitive decline. 

Phosphorous: Helps with kidney function, nerve signalling and maintaining a normal heartbeat.

Sodium: Used in the body to control blood pressure and volume and ensures muscles and nerves work properly. 

Protein: When digested releases amino acids which are converted into hormones like adrenalin which can be used as energy for the body.

Contains NO sugar .
